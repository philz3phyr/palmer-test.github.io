Who We Are

The company is backed by its president's extensive experience in international trade , project finance , business consulting and management. The company is involved in project financing and capital sourcing for landmark projects in Ghana , Liberia , Ivory Coast and Sierra Leone just to mention a few .

With the addition of subsidiaries like , Palmer Energy , Palmer Petroleum and Palmer Capital , the Palmer Group of companies is your Partner 4 Results that offers a one stop shop for a solution to your business in the 21st Century and beyond.

Palmer Group LLC is also dedicated to giving back to our less privileged people in our communities. We support the West African Humanitarian Organization and are active members of the Ghana Advocacy Group and Clean Ghana Army.

Palmer Group also partners with local and foreign charitably organizations to donate clothes, shoes,and small medical equipment. We also facilitate a My-Health drive where we teach people basic preventive health measures like CPR & First Aid.

Agriculture

Paulonia Plantation - Afforestation Project: the cultivation of Paulownia; also known as Empress Plant. A fast growing specie of timber over an area covering 2000 acres over a period of four years. At Bonyere in the Jomoro Municipal District of the Western Region of the Republic of Ghana

  • Bell Pepper Farming - 500 acres of Bell Pepper farming mainly for export Europe and it is located in Koforidua, Easter Region of Ghana.
  • Maize Farming - This initiative is part of the Government of Ghana Food for Jobs Program and also The One District One Factory Project. The project is in 3 Phases. Phase one which is currently ongoing seeks to the Plantation of 500 acres of maize.
  • Phase two involves and upscale in farming with an additional 4,500 acres to be added after a certain period. Phase 3 will see the introduction of a factory for fast processing and storage of the maize which consistent all year round supplies to the value adding industries in Ghana and the East African Region
